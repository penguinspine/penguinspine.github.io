ABOUT


ProtoStats aims to revolutionize the way statistics are learned. Our team consists of passionate individuals who have a deep understanding of statistics and a strong commitment to education.

Statistics is often seen as challenging by students due to its association with mathematics and lack of effective learning tools. This can lead to difficulties in interpreting and analyzing data, as well as forming informed judgments. At Sta. Maria Integrated High School, there is a growing concern about the lack of attention given to teaching ICT. Grade 11 students often struggle with understanding concepts and calculations, leading to frustration and a lack of confidence in applying statistical concepts to real-life scenarios.


Researchers plan to develop a website to facilitate students learning statistics, highlighting the importance of ICT skills in scientific disciplines and everyday decision-making. This initiative can encourage schools to prioritize digital literacy and provide students with the necessary tools and knowledge for the digital age.
